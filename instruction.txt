load-modal

 The purpoese of this website is to visualise 3D buildings and flood in central Tokyo area with immersive user experience. 
 
 Buildings on this website are in 3D Tiles format, representing buildings in Chiyoda ward of LOD1 and LOD2. On this website, users are allowed to ajust water level, view buildings' 
 inundation level and explore the area in first person view. Moreover, the user can experience all functions above in VR (virtual reality) Mode.
